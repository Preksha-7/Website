ABOUT
Suresh Raina is an Indian International Player and among India's finest T20 batsmen.Typical left-hander's elegance, power game and a fine temperament were the hallmark of Raina. He has made 5615 ODI runs and 1604 T20I runs. He has picked 36 wickets in his ODI career and 13 wickets in his T20I career.
