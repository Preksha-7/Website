ABOUT:
Sourav Ganguly is an Indian player who has established himself as one of the leading batsmen in the world. He started to open the innings along with Sachin Tendulkar in ODIs. The duo formed what was arguably the most dangerous opening partnerships in ODI cricket history.
He has made 11360 ODI runs and 7234 Test runs
