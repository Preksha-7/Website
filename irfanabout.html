Irfan Pathan is an Indian cricketer and is regarded as India's next best all-rounder to Kapil Dev.Irfan swings the ball both ways and troubled almost every batsman at his peek. He has 173 ODI wickets and 100 test wickets on his name.He has made 1544 ODI runs and 1115 Test runs.
