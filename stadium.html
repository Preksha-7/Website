Narendra Modi Stadium Lightshow During IPL 2023, Ahmedabad Sky Lit Up by Drones

Narendra Modi Stadium hosted the inaugural Indian Premier League (IPL) 2023 match between Chennai Super Kings and Gujarat Titans in Ahmedabad on March 31. The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) had big plans for the 16th edition of IPL, and after a star-studded ceremony, the apex cricket board lit up the Ahmedabad sky with a stunning drone show.

The drones all came together to form the TATA IPL logo, followed by the figures of a bowler and then a batter striking the ball.
