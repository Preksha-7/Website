ABOUT:
Rahul Dravid is an indian cricketer regarded as one of the most consistent players in the history of the game. He has scored 13,288 ODI runs and 10,889 test runs. Rahul Dravid was probably one of the last classical Test match batters, and one of the greatest.Armed with an orthodox technique, he became the cement that held the foundations firm while the flair players expressed themselves.
