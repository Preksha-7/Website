Right handed Batsman

ABOUT:
Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar is an Indian international cricketer. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen cricket has ever seen with an unbeatable record of 18426 runs in ODI and 15921 Test runs. He holds the record for recieving the most 'man of the match' awards in international cricket. Along with that,he holds the record of 100 centuries in international cricket which is highest by any player till now.
