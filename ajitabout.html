ABOUT:
Ajit Agarkar is an Indian cricketer and the third highest wicket-taker for India in ODI. He is also the fastest to bag 50 ODI wickets, the fastest in terms of matches to complete a double of 200 wickets and 1000 runs, fastest fifty by an Indian, in just 21 balls. He is known to swing the ball both ways, both with the new and the old balls. He has taken 288 ODI wickets and has 1296 ODI runs.
